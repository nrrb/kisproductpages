Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Yoga 2nd Gen

$2241.00

Tablet/Laptop Convertible

The next generation of the ThinkPad X1 Yoga Tablet/Laptop convertible, this one touts all updated parts while slightly decreasing the weight and thickness of the previous generation. This is configured with an Intel i7-7600 3.6 GHz CPU, 16 GB RAM, 512 GB SSD, 802.11ac Wi-Fi, 1920x1080 FHD screen with Pen and Touch input, and 3 year Accidental Damage Protection (ADP) warranty*.

The tablet functionality is great for annotating PowerPoint slides and PDFs during teaching, presentations, and note-taking. This functions like a laptop, and converts into a tablet by simply rotating the screen back until the keyboard is behind it. The keys on the keyboard recess into the laptop body to prevent accidental keypresses.

* The Accidental Damage Protection (ADP) Warranty

The ADP warranty is standard on all of our laptops, providing complete coverage in the case of accidental damage as well as manufacturer defects. We began including this on our Lenovo laptop offerings several years ago, in response to costly repairs due to the inevitable accidents that occur in the hectic lifestyles of business school participants.
