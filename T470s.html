Lenovo ThinkPad T470s

Previous Next

Description

The ThinkPad T470s maximizes performance while reducing cost at the slight expense of portability (as compared to the X1-series laptops). Still, at under 3/4" thick and under 3 lb in weight, this is extremely portable. The model that KIS is recommending has a touch screen. With the ThinkPad Ultra Dock, this laptop supports two 4K monitors. If you need to eke out as much memory as possible, the RAM is upgradeable to 24 GB. For quick recovery in the case of hardware problems, KIS is focused on stocking loaner shells of this type.

Highlights

  • Lower price point for same maximized feature set as X1-series laptops.
  • Less than 3/4" thick and weighs less than 3 lb.
  • RAM upgradeable to 24 GB.
  • 3 year Accidental Damage Protection warranty covers all accidental damage, including water damage and drops.

Specifications

  • Weight: 2.9 lb (1.32 kg)
  • Size (WxDxH): 13.03" x 8.93" x 0.67-0.74"; 331mm x 226.8mm x 16.9-18.8mm
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-7600U 2.80 GHz (2 cores, 4 with HT)
  • RAM: 16 GB (upgradeable to 24 GB)
  • Storage: 512 GB SSD (upgradeable to 1 TB)
  • Display: 14" 1920x1080 resolution (FHD; 250 nits brightness; Touch)
  • Battery: 3-cell (23 Wh) + 3-cell (26 Wh) (up to 10.5 hours; internal, not replaceable)
  • Warranty: 3 year Accidental Damage Protection
