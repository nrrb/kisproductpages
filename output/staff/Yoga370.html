Lenovo ThinkPad Yoga 370

Previous Next

Description

This Lenovo ThinkPad Yoga 370 is the workhorse tablet/laptop convertible at Kellogg, the successor to the tablet used by our teaching support team.

Highlights

  • Convertible between tablet and laptop modes by simply hinging the screen backwards.
  • Tablet interaction works with both the high-precision stylus and with 10-point Touch sensitivity.
  • 3 year Accidental Damage Protection warranty covers all accidental damage, including water damage and drops.

Specifications

  • Weight: 3.03 lb (1.37 kg)
  • Size (WxDxH): 12.34" x 8.75" x 0.72"; 313.5mm x 222.2mm x 18.2mm
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-7600U 2.80 GHz (2 cores, 4 with HT)
  • RAM: 16 GB
  • Storage: 512 GB SSD
  • Display: 13.3" 1920x1080 resolution (FHD; 300 nits brightness; Touch and Pen)
  • Battery: 4-cell 51 Wh (up to 12.5 hours)
  • Warranty: 3 year Accidental Damage Protection
