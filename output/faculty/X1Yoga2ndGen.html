Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Yoga (2nd Gen)

Previous Next

Description

This Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Yoga is the second generation of a very popular and successful tablet at Kellogg. Popular with presenters, note-takers, people who like using touch interaction, or who just want to try something new.

Highlights

  • Thinnest (0.69") and lightest (3.15 lb) full-size ultraportable laptop/tablet convertible available.
  • Tablet interaction works with both the high-precision stylus and with 10-point Touch sensitivity.
  • 3 year Accidental Damage Protection warranty covers all accidental damage, including water damage and drops.

Specifications

  • Weight: 3.15 lb (1.42 kg)
  • Size (WxDxH): 13.11" x 9.02" x 0.69"; 333mm x 229mm x 17.4mm
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-7600U 2.80 GHz (2 cores, 4 with HT)
  • RAM: 16 GB
  • Storage: 512 GB SSD (upgradeable to 1 TB)
  • Display: 14" 1920x1080 resolution (FHD; 270 nits brightness; Touch and Pen)
  • Battery: 4-cell 56 Wh (up to 15.5 hours)
  • Warranty: 3 year Accidental Damage Protection
